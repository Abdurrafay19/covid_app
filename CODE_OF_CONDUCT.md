# Code of Conduct

Welcome to the project! This is a small, personally maintained repository, and I am super excited to have people check out the code, open issues, or submit pull requests. 

To keep things running smoothly, I just ask that everyone follows a few basic ground rules:

* **Be respectful:** Treat everyone with kindness and empathy.
* **Be constructive:** If you are reporting a bug or reviewing code, focus on the problem, not the person. Provide clear, reproducible steps or helpful feedback.
* **Keep it welcoming:** Harassment, discriminatory language, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated, and offending comments or PRs will be closed immediately.

If you experience any issues or need to reach out privately, you can contact me directly. 

Thanks for contributing!
